# Phase 2: User Personas

## Persona 1: Arjun — Engineering Student

**Age:** 21
**Education:** 3rd Year Computer Science
**Location:** Bangalore, India
**Device:** Laptop with 8GB RAM, integrated Intel GPU
**Internet:** Unreliable hostel WiFi

**Goals:**
- Master DSA, system design, and machine learning concepts
- Prepare for campus placements and coding interviews
- Organize 50+ PDF textbooks and research papers

**Pain Points:**
- Spends 3+ hours daily creating notes and flashcards manually
- Forgets concepts after 2 weeks — no spaced repetition
- Juggles Chrome tabs + Obsidian + Anki + ChatGPT
- PDFs from Indian publishers have poor OCR quality

**Daily Workflow:**
```
9am: Downloads lecture PDF
10am: Reads, highlights manually
12pm: Types notes into Obsidian
2pm: Creates Anki cards (30 min)
4pm: Practices coding
9pm: Realizes he forgot morning's topic
```

**Needs:**
- One-click PDF → structured notes
- Auto-generated Anki-compatible flashcards
- Works on unreliable internet
- Lightweight — can't afford high-end laptop

**Quote:**
> "I spend more time organizing my study material than actually studying."
